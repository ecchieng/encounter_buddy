How to Use

1. Enter an age into the box.

2. Click "Add Number" or hit "Enter" on your keyboard to add it to the list.

3. Click "Merge PDFs" to generate the encounter sheet.

4. Use "Remove Last" to undo a mistake.

5. Use "Reset" to clear everything.

Entering Ages

1. Weeks are abbreviated as "w" so 2 weeks = 2w

2. Months are abbreviated as "mo" so 9 month = 9mo

3. Years are not required to be entered so 5 years = 5
